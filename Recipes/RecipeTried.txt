 Ingredients: 
 Tomato Paste 
 Parmesan 
 Pasta 
 Garlic 
 Water 
 oil 
 pesto 
 
 Instructions: 
 Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9x13 inch baking dish. In a large bowl, combine all ingredients except salt, pepper, garlic, onion and 1/2 teaspoon oregano. Mix well and season with salt. Bake in the preheated oven until cheese melts and cheese is golden, about 30 minutes. Cool. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
